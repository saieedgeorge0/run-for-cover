Run for Cover is the University of Chicago's award-winning historically all-male a cappella group. Founded in 2007 by a group of 5 students, it has grown to become one of the prominent all-male groups in the Midwest. The group performs at events both within and outside of the university, and competed in the International Championship of Collegiate A Cappella in 2014, advancing to the Semifinal round. Run for Cover recently released its sophomore album, as well as a new single, which you can find on iTunes and Spotify, along with the rest of their recorded tracks. Our track Krwlng also just won runner-up for best male collegiate song at the CARAs. This year, the group also competed in the ICCAs for the first time since Jan 2016, and we will be releasing our set in the fall as an EP. We always welcome new gigs and would love to sing for you - visit our contact us page in order to get in touch!
